# Meeting Output Template

## Purpose
Standardize what CD should produce from a meeting transcript, recording, or note source (for example Fireflies output).

The point is to turn meetings into:
- clear notes
- decisions
- actions
- follow-ups
- dashboard-visible state

---

## 1) Meeting header
- **Meeting title:**
- **Date/time:**
- **Platform/source:**
- **Participants:**
- **Source link:**

## 2) Executive summary
A short plain-language summary of what the meeting was actually about.

## 3) Key decisions
List only real decisions.
Not every topic discussed.

Format:
- decision
- implication

## 4) Key takeaways
What matters most coming out of the meeting?

These can include:
- strategic implications
- risks
- blockers
- clarified understanding

## 5) Action items
For each action:
- owner
- action
- due date / timing if known
- destination type:
  - reminder
  - task
  - project backlog
  - follow-up draft

## 6) Follow-up queue
Items that need a response, check-in, clarification, or next-step outreach.

For each:
- who/what
- why follow-up is needed
- timing

## 7) Open questions
Questions raised but not resolved.

## 8) Shareable recap
A concise version that could be sent to partners or teams if needed.

---

## Output modes

### Private version
May include:
- sharper commentary
- risk signals
- candid operator notes

### Shareable version
Should focus on:
- summary
- decisions
- action items
- next steps

---

## Rule
If a meeting output does not produce decisions, actions, follow-ups, or clarified understanding, it is probably too fluffy to be useful.
