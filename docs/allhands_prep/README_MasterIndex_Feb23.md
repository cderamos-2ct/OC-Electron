## IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG

### Attendee challenges a fact
→ "Let me verify that and follow up with you this week."  
→ Don't argue in the moment. You have the data; confirm after.

### Time runs short
→ Skip Presario/Triangle/POA; stick to: Clarity, Klippa, the 3 core asks, decision handout.  
→ Hand out decision handout; ask for written confirmations by EOD Friday.

### Someone is clearly unhappy
→ "I hear you. Let me take that offline with [their peer] and we'll circle back."  
→ Don't try to convince them in front of everyone. 1-on-1 conversation works better.

### Projector fails
→ Switch to printed documents; walk through them manually. You're covered.

### Decision-making stalls
→ "Let's capture this as DEFERRED to [date]. John, can you own the follow-up?"  
→ Keep the meeting moving; resolve offline.
