## CONTINGENCY PLANS

**If projector fails:**
- [ ] Have PDF on USB ready to display on laptop
- [ ] Have printed slides (6 copies) as backup
- [ ] Presenter can use laptop screen if needed

**If you blank on a talking point:**
- [ ] Pause, take a breath, reference your speaker notes
- [ ] Say "Let me pull this up" (redirect to handout or notes)
- [ ] Don't apologize; just move forward

**If an attendee is hostile/skeptical:**
- [ ] Listen fully without interrupting
- [ ] Acknowledge their concern ("That's a fair point")
- [ ] Refer to data/evidence ("Here's what our testing shows")
- [ ] If you don't know the answer, offer to follow up ("Let me research that and get back to you")

**If you run over on time:**
- [ ] Skip Slide 14 appendix (save for follow-ups)
- [ ] Compress Q&A slightly, but don't rush attendees
- [ ] Better to end 5 min late with good questions answered than rush and leave ambiguity

**If a key attendee is absent:**
- [ ] Still proceed (you need at least 3 partners to make decisions)
- [ ] Note their absence in follow-up communications
- [ ] Schedule 1:1 catch-up with absent attendee within 48 hours
