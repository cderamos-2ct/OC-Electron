## THE FOUR CRITICAL MOMENTS

**You MUST land these:**

### Moment 1: Slide 5 (Klippa Risk)
**What to say:** "Klippa works for simple cases. But we're not building for simple cases. We're building for manufacturers with 50-page specs, law firms with tables and amendments. And here's the accountability problem: they have no obligation to fix these things."

**Pause.** Let them feel the constraint.

**Reference:** `AllHands_SpeakerNotes_Feb23.md` (Slide 5 section)

---

### Moment 2: Slide 6 (60% vs 95%)
**What to say:** "Here's what we've been testing: Claude-based extraction on the same documents that Klippa fails on. The results: 95% confidence. Let me repeat that—95% confidence on documents where Klippa was at 60%."

**Pause.** Let the number land.

**Reference:** `AllHands_SpeakerNotes_Feb23.md` (Slide 6 section)

---

### Moment 3: Slide 11 (4 Decisions)
**What to say:** "I'm going to be very specific here. We have four asks—one per domain. These are not nice-to-haves. They determine how fast we scale."

**Then go one by one.** Be direct. No ambiguity.

**Reference:** `AllHands_SpeakerNotes_Feb23.md` (Slide 11 section)

---

### Moment 4: Slide 12 (Your Role)
**What to say:** "If we get these decisions and resources, I'm ready to expand my scope. I want to own the platform roadmap and the vendor relationships. That's where I can add the most value. Let's build this together."

**Confidence, not hesitation.**
