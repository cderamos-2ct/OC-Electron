## PHRASES TO USE & PHRASES TO AVOID

### USE (Confidence-Building)
- "That's a great question. Here's how we're handling it..."
- "I've thought through that. The answer is..."
- "Kyle and I are aligned on this."
- "Let me bring John/Eric in on that since it's their domain..."
- "By Mar 31, we'll have a decision on that."
- "The data shows..."
- "This is a business decision, not just technical."

### AVOID (Uncertainty-Creating)
- "I think" (say "I know" or "Our data shows")
- "We'll figure it out" (too vague)
- "Probably" (commit or don't)
- "We hope to" (weak)
- "It should work out" (no confidence)
- "Maybe" (no)
- Defensive tone (own the problem, own the solution)
